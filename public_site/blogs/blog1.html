Top Resume Mistakes to Avoid

Published on May 22, 2025

Your resume is the first impression you make on a potential employer. Avoiding these common mistakes can help your resume stand out:

1. Typos and grammatical errors: Always proofread and consider using tools like Grammarly.

2. Generic language: Tailor your resume to the job you're applying for. Use specific metrics and impact-driven results.

3. Irrelevant experience: Focus on showcasing experience that’s relevant to the role.

4. Poor formatting: Keep the layout clean and professional, and avoid overly decorative fonts.

5. Missing keywords: Use job description keywords to pass applicant tracking systems (ATS).

By avoiding these mistakes, your resume can better represent your qualifications and increase your chances of landing an interview.
